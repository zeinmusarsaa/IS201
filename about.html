About Me

This is what I do.

Born on December 13th, 2001 under the harsh conditions of war, my mother gave birth to me with only a nurse present at the same second a missile landed on a building directly opposite from the hospital.

My parents called me a miracle, to have survived the conditions of my birth with no difficulties in my health, or hearing. After I was old enough to listen to my birth story, I realized that I am no different than any Palestinian born during war, and in fact, I was lucky to have a family that wanted to give me everything in order to see me succeed.

Despite the traumatic experiences of growing up during wars, and seeing things no kid has to, I do admit that compared to many Palestinian families, I am privileged. And in my privilege, I hope to show Palestine in its true colors through this website
